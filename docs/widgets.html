Widgets catalog

What exists today, what it’s good for, and what to avoid.

Button

ui9_button_draw(ui, r, "Primary", Ui9Primary, Ui9StateNormal);

Use your app to track press/release; don’t hide logic inside the widget.

Toggle

ui9_toggle_draw(ui, r, "Wi-Fi", on);

Slider

ui9_slider_draw(ui, r, v);
v = ui9_slider_value(r, mouse.xy);

Segmented control (3-way)

ui9_segment3_draw(ui, segR, labels, sel);
sel = ui9_segment3_hit(segR, mouse.xy);

Text field

ui9_textfield_draw(ui, r, runebuf, nelem(runebuf), focused, "(type)");

List item

ui9_listitem_draw(ui, r, "Display", selected);

Progress

ui9_progress_draw(ui, r, pct);

Dropdown pieces

ui9_dropdown_btn_draw(ui, r, "Theme: Default", open);
ui9_dropdown_item_draw(ui, itemR, "Default", selected);
The menu container & selection logic live in your app (or a future higher-level widget).
